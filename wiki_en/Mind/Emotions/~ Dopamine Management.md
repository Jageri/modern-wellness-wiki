# Dopamine Management

**Mortality Impact**: No direct mortality data, but strongly associated with addiction, depression, and impulsive behavior—mortality risk factors
**Evidence Level**: ⭐⭐ (Well-established mechanisms; insufficient intervention evidence)
**Actionability**: High

## Conclusion
Dopamine isn't the "happiness molecule"—it's the "anticipation molecule"—it drives pursuit, not satisfaction. Modern life (social media, short videos, gambling, junk food) constantly hijacks the dopamine system, leading to rising pleasure thresholds and declining motivation. Managing dopamine isn't about eliminating it, but about avoiding overstimulation.

## Evidence
- Andrew Huberman's dopamine framework: Baseline dopamine levels matter more than peaks—frequent peaks cause baseline down-regulation (i.e., "feeling good now, feeling broken later")
- Addiction mechanisms: All addictive substances/behaviors act through dopamine pathways; repeated stimulation causes receptor downregulation
- Delayed gratification experiments: Those who can resist immediate dopamine stimulation (the famous marshmallow experiment) have better long-term health and income
- Social media/short videos: Dopamine pulses every 15 seconds → attention fragmentation → reduced deep thinking ability
- Relationship to depression: Overly low dopamine baseline → motivation loss → anhedonia

## Do
1. **Reduce high-frequency, low-quality stimulation**: Reduce mindless scrolling of short videos and social media
2. **Dopamine fast**: Choose 1 day per week to completely avoid electronic entertainment devices
3. **Exercise**: The healthiest dopamine source, and doesn't cause receptor downregulation
4. **Cold exposure**: Cold showers can briefly increase dopamine 2–3× (lasting several hours)
5. **Don't do multiple "pleasurable" things simultaneously**: Eating while scrolling phone while listening to podcast → dopamine peak too high → each activity done separately feels boring
6. **Accept boredom**: Boredom is a signal that dopamine baseline is recovering, not a problem to solve
